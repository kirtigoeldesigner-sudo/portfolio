Available Services

01

UX Workshops

Developed a prototype but unsure if the experience lands? I facilitate deep-dive workshops to analyse user flows, identify pain points, and map out detailed journeys that elevate overall experience.

02

Feature Enhancement

Looking to expand your feature set and customer journey? I design enhancements that integrate seamlessly into existing flows — coherent, intentional, and invisible in the best way.

03

Visual Design Language

Your flow is solid but the product doesn't feel right yet. I help carve a distinctive visual niche — crafting a design language that resonates and differentiates your product.

04

Product Usage Metrics

Struggling to scale while keeping acquisition costs down? I identify key product metrics — activation rate, retention, feature adoption — and the strategies to move them.

05

Usage & Behaviour Analysis

Have a few thousand users and want to understand them deeply? I review analytics to uncover drop-off points, underutilised features, and friction in the intended user journey.

06

Executive Dashboards

Power users need dashboards that make dense data legible at a glance. I design effective, beautiful dashboards with careful consideration of overview vs. detail — for the decisions that matter.
