About Kirti

I believe UX is the key differentiator in any product.

I am a UX leader with 15+ years of experience creating intuitive and engaging user experiences for enterprise as well as consumer products. I also consult tech startups to align them with their business and user goals.

How technology shapes human behaviour — and how human behaviour, in turn, affects design choices — is what drives my curiosity. On similar lines, I'm deeply interested in how Generative AI shall reshape the way we interact with systems entirely.
